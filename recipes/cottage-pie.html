Cottage Pie

Nostalgic homemade cottage pie in under an hour!

Ingredients:

  • 1lb seasoned ground beef
  • 1 16oz can baked beans
  • 4 large russet potatoes-whipped with butter, cream, salt, and pepper to taste
  • 1 block Velveeta cheese
  • Your favorite hot sauce to taste(optional)

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Boil potatoes until soft
  3. Cook ground beef in skillet and season to taste
  4. Strain potatoes and whip with butter, cream, salt, and pepper to taste
  5. Strain and chop ground beef
  6. Add beef to a large pot
  7. Strain baked beans and layer on top of beef
  8. Slice half of the Velveeta block and layer on top of beans
  9. Layer whipped potatoes over cheese
  10. Slice the remaining cheese and layer on top of potatoes
  11. Cover pot and cook in oven until cheese is melted
  12. Enjoy!
