Chocolate milkshake

Ice cold and creamy chocolate milkshake!

Ingredients:

  • 3 scoops of chocolate ice cream
  • 4oz condensed milk
  • 4oz whole milk
  • Whipped cream
  • Fresh cherry for garnishing(optional)

Steps:

  1. Add ice cream to blender
  2. Add condensed and whole milk to blender
  3. Blend until smooth and creamy consistency
  4. Pour into frosted glass
  5. Garnish and enjoy!
