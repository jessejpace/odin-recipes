Ahi Tuna Bites

Lite and fresh ahi tuna bites!

Ingredients:

  • 5 cucumber slices
  • 4oz ahi tuna steak
  • Pickled ginger and peppadew pepper relish
  • Sriracha sauce
  • Fresh micro-greens for garnishing(optional)

Steps:

  1. Decorate plate with Sriracha sauce
  2. Place cucumber slices
  3. Blacken and sear tuna steak
  4. Slice tuna steak and place on top of cucumber slices
  5. Add a pinch of ginger and peppadew relish on top of each piece of tuna each
  6. Garnish and enjoy!
