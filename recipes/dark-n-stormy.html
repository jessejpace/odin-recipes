Dark-N-Stormy

Delicious and refreshing Dark-N-Stormy cocktail!

Ingredients:

  • 1.5oz Gosling's Dark Rum
  • 0.5oz fresh lime juice
  • 6oz Gosling's Ginger Beer
  • 3 dashes of Angostura Aromatic Bitters
  • Fresh lime wheel for garnishing(optional)

Steps:

  1. Add ice to a copper mug or cocktail glass of your choosing
  2. Measure and pour in rum
  3. Measure and pour in lime juice
  4. Top with ginger beer
  5. Finish with 3 dashes of bitters
  6. Garnish and enjoy!
